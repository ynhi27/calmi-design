Our Product

What is CALMI?

CALMI is a grief companion designed for remembrance—not to heal or replace grief, but to help you keep your loved ones present. It's a companion that supports you in preserving their stories, their voice, and your connection to them over time.

Who Is CALMI For?

Anyone who has lost someone significant and wants a gentle, ongoing way to remember them—whether the loss was recent or years ago. CALMI is for people who wish they could still talk to that person, ask for advice, or simply feel their presence.

How CALMI Helps

CALMI helps you maintain a lasting connection through conversation, memories, and rituals. It's there when you want to remember, reflect, or feel close to someone you've lost—on your terms, in your own space.

How CALMI Works

01

A Safe Conversation Space

Users interact with CALMI through a chat-based interface, similar to messaging apps they already use daily. There is no pressure, no expectation — just space to talk.

02

Emotion Recognition

CALMI uses natural language processing to understand emotional tone — such as sadness, confusion, guilt, or anger — and responds with supportive, age-appropriate guidance.

03

Guided Reflection

Instead of giving advice, CALMI gently asks questions that encourage reflection, helping users process their emotions in a healthy and constructive way.

04

Resource Suggestions

When needed, CALMI suggests coping exercises, journaling prompts, breathing techniques, or professional resources appropriate for young people.
