Our Product

What is CALMI?

CALMI is a grief bot designed for remembrance—not to heal or replace grief, but to help you keep your loved ones present. It’s a companion that supports you in preserving their stories, their voice, and your connection to them over time.

Who Is It For?

Anyone who has lost someone significant and wants a gentle, ongoing way to remember them—whether the loss was recent or years ago. CALMI is for people who wish they could still talk to that person, ask for advice, or simply feel their presence.

How It Helps

CALMI helps you maintain a lasting connection through conversation, memories, and rituals. It’s there when you want to remember, reflect, or feel close to someone you’ve lost—on your terms, in your own space.
