About the Project

GriefBot is a research-driven AI voice chatbot designed to support individuals experiencing loss. The system focuses on emotional reflection, memory sharing, and healthy coping rather than replacing loved ones or providing therapy.

Key Features

Voice Interaction

Warm, calm AI-generated voice responses for emotional comfort.

Grief-Aware Dialogues

Conversation flows informed by psychological research.

Ethical Boundaries

Clear limits to prevent impersonation and dependency.

Privacy Focus

No storage of sensitive personal voice data.

Research & Design Process

Literature Review

Study of grief models, mental health AI, and ethical frameworks.

User Studies

Interviews and surveys to understand emotional needs.

Prototyping

Iterative design using wireframes and voice simulations.

Prototype Demonstration

This prototype demonstrates how GriefBot responds to emotional input using scripted and AI-supported dialogues.

Sample Interaction:

"I miss her every day."

"I'm really sorry you're feeling this pain. Would you like to share a memory that feels meaningful to you?"

Ethical Principles

  • Transparency: The system clearly identifies as AI.
  • Non-Impersonation: No simulation of specific individuals.
  • User Autonomy: Encourages external support and self-care.
  • Privacy Protection: No personal voice storage.
  • Emotional Safety: Predefined boundary responses.
