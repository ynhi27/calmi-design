The Problem

Many teenagers and young adults experience grief during important stages of their lives. Despite living in a highly connected digital world, many still struggle to talk openly about emotional pain.

Students often hesitate to share their grief with family, friends, or counselors because they fear being judged or do not want to burden others.

Our Approach

Our team explored how digital tools could help bridge the emotional support gap for young people. Through research and surveys, we investigated how students cope with loss and whether they would feel comfortable interacting with an AI companion.
